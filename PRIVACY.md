# VitalsOS Privacy Policy

*Last updated: June 26, 2026*

VitalsOS ("the app") is a personal habit and mission tracker with gamification. This policy describes what data the app handles.

## Summary

- **We do not operate a server** for your habit data.
- **We do not sell your data.**
- **We do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers** in the current version.

## Data stored on your device

The app stores the following locally on your iPhone or iPad (SwiftData):

- Mission completions and logs
- Character progress (XP, level, pillars, vitals)
- Mission and category templates you activate
- Optional profile photo you choose
- App settings (theme, reminders, titles, effects)
- Quit Challenge progress

This data stays on your device. To move data to a new iPhone, use an **iCloud or computer backup** when you set up the new device (Apple handles this — not VitalsOS servers).

VitalsOS does **not** sync habit data between two active devices in v1. Each device keeps its own copy unless you restore from an Apple device backup.

## Permissions

| Permission | Why |
|------------|-----|
| **Photos (read)** | Optional: choose a profile photo on the Character tab |
| **Photos (add)** | Optional: save Stats or Character share images to your library |
| **Notifications** | Optional: local daily reminders you configure in Manage → Reminders |

You can deny permissions; core tracking still works without photos or notifications.

## iCloud / device backup

If you back up your iPhone to iCloud or a computer, Apple may include app data in that backup. That is controlled by Apple’s backup settings, not by VitalsOS servers.

## Subscriptions (future)

If you purchase a subscription, billing is handled by **Apple** via the App Store. We may use **RevenueCat** to validate subscription status on device. RevenueCat and Apple process purchase receipts according to their own privacy policies.

## Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy. The latest version will be published in this repository.

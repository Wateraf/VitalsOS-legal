# How to Play VitalsOS

VitalsOS turns real habits into **XP**, **six traits**, and **vitals** for your character. Everything runs on **honest self-reporting** — tick what you actually did.

---

## The daily loop

1. Open **Daily** or **Missions** and tick what you completed today.  
2. Earn **XP** toward your level.  
3. Boost **traits** (up to **+2 per trait per day** from routine missions).  
4. Refill **vitals** with daily basics (meals, sleep, hygiene, water).  
5. Check **Character** for progress and **Stats** for streaks.

---

## Daily vs Missions

| Tab | What it’s for |
|-----|----------------|
| **Daily** | Everyday basics — meals, sleep, shower, water, and **Quit Challenge** when active. |
| **Missions** | Longer habits grouped by category — sport, study, wellness, willpower, and more. |

Add your own tasks with **+** or activate templates from **Manage → Template Library**.

---

## XP & daily cap

- Most missions award **XP** when ticked.  
- **Daily mission XP is capped at 350** per day (bonuses below are separate).  
- After you hit the cap, XP shown can still count up (e.g. 400/350) so your log stays honest, but **level XP stops** until the next day.  
- **Bad habit** missions (Missions tab, optional) **deduct XP** when you admit a slip. They do **not** change traits.

---

## Six traits

Each routine mission tags one trait:

| Trait | Examples |
|-------|----------|
| **Vitality** | Exercise, movement |
| **Wisdom** | Study, deep work |
| **Calm** | Meditation, rest |
| **Bond** | Friends, family |
| **Order** | Chores, daily routine |
| **Resolve** | Willpower, saying no |

You can gain **up to +2 per trait per day** from missions. Extra ticks still count in the background but won’t add more than +2 to that trait until the day resets.

---

## Vitals

Daily essentials (meals, shower, sleep, water, etc.) refill **Hunger**, **Hygiene**, **Energy**, **Fun**, **Social**, and **Health**. Keep them up so your character stays in good shape. Vitals decay slowly if you do nothing.

**Sleep tip:** Log sleep when you **wake up**, not at bedtime — you'll know how many hours you slept, and **Energy** refills from that total (about **7 hours** for a full bar).

---

## Day reset (4:00 AM)

Your “game day” **resets at 4:00 AM local time**, not midnight.

- Daily XP cap and trait limits refresh.  
- Missions can be ticked again.  
- **Login bonus** can be claimed once per game day.

If the app is open when the day rolls over, the login screen appears automatically.

---

## Login bonus

The first time each game day, you get a **login reward** (does **not** count toward the 350 mission XP cap):

| Streak | Bonus |
|--------|--------|
| Every day | +10 XP |
| Every 7 days | +50 XP |
| Every 30 days | +300 XP and +4 to all traits |

Miss a game day and your streak restarts.

---

## Quit Challenge

On **Daily**, start a challenge to break a bad habit (7 → 30 → 90 days).

- Tap **“I succeeded today”** every game day.  
- Missing a day counts as a relapse (XP penalty and lockout).  
- Clear tiers for **XP and trait rewards** (these bonuses bypass the daily mission XP cap).  
- While a challenge is **active**, it appears at the **top** of Daily so you don’t forget to check in.

---

## Discipline & Willpower vs Bad Habits

| | **Discipline & Willpower** | **Bad Habits** |
|--|---------------------------|----------------|
| Mindset | “I resisted / did the hard thing today” | “I slipped — logging honestly” |
| XP | Positive | Penalty |
| Traits | +Resolve when ticked | No trait change |

Pick templates from **Manage → Template Library** that match how you want to track.

---

## Stats & sharing

**Stats** shows completions, active days, and a calendar. Use **Share to Photos** on Stats or Character to save a story card.

**Focus** in Stats only lists **active** missions and categories so the list stays short.

---

## Character style

**Character → Style** lets you choose **login streak** and **quit challenge** badge effects on your avatar. (Wearable title collection is simplified in early versions.)

---

## Backup (new phone)

**Manage → Data & Backup → Export backup** saves an encrypted copy of your progress. **Import backup** on another iPhone restores it. Subscription is separate — use **Restore Purchases** after import.

---

## Play honest

VitalsOS only works if you’re honest with yourself. Tick what you truly did — skipping the truth skips real progress. The app won’t verify your gym visit or your screen time; **you** are the source of truth.

---

## Tips

- Hide missions you don’t need (swipe on a row) instead of deleting templates.  
- Use **timed** missions to log minutes; use **unit** missions for things like water (liters).  
- Enable **Manage → Reminders** for morning/evening nudges.
